SAFETY INFORMATION

Indications and clinical use:

TactuPump™ Forte (adapalene 0.3%0.1%/benzoyl peroxide 2.5%) Topical Gel is indicated for treatment of moderate and severe acne vulgaris, characterized by comedones, inflammatory papules/pustules with or without occasional nodules in patients 12 years of age and older.

TactuPump™ Forte may be considered for patients who have risk factors that worsen acne prognosis, those who are prone to or at risk for scarring and those who may have intolerance or contraindication to systemic treatment.

Safety and effectiveness have not been established in geriatric patients (≥65 years).

Contraindication:

  • Application to areas of skin affected by eczema or seborrheic dermatitis

Most serious warnings and precautions:

  • For external use only, not for ophthalmic use
  • Should not be used by pregnant women: use only after contraceptive counselling in women of childbearing age

Other relevant warnings and precautions:

  • Avoid eyes, lips, angles of the nose, mucous membranes, abraded skin and open wounds
  • May bleach hair and coloured fabric; caution when applying near hairline
  • Not recommended with concomitant topical acne therapy or potentially irritating topical products
  • Avoid electrolysis, “waxing” and chemical depilatories for hair removal on skin treated with TactuPump™ or TactuPump™ Forte
  • Patients should be advised to use non-comedogenic cosmetics
  • Certain cutaneous signs and symptoms can be expected with use
  • Avoid excessive sunlight, including sunlamps (avoid exposure or use protection, avoid sunburned skin); weather extremes, such as wind or cold, may be irritating
  • Caution in nursing women
